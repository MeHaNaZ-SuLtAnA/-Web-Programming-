About Us

WELCOME TO HIMCHORI RESORT. EXPERIENTIAL HOSPITALITY AT ITS FINEST

Himchori Resorts is a premier hotel and resort destination of Bangladesh. Its different properties in the northeast district of Sylhet offer places to stay in the tea garden, forest and adventure locations. Built in eco-friendly environments, Himchori Resorts offers more than a retreat; it provides a sanctuary amidst nature. With a multitude of activities, many sights to see and places to visit, Himchori Resorts is the ultimate destination for guests who love nature.
